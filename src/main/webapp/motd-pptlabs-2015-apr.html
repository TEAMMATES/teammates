PowerPointLabs: Another useful product from the TEAMMATES team.

PowerPointLabs is a free PowerPoint add-in for creating better slides with less effort.

Features: Zoom and pan animations, generate motion path animations by specifying start and end positions, create spotlights, magnifying glass effect, highlight bullet points, generate audio narrations from slide notes, and more...
